About NH High Peaks

NH High Peaks was inspired by hikers, trail runners, and mountain enthusiasts. The website was built to help you keep track of the NH 4000 footers you have summited, view the routes you took to get there, and plan your next adventure. To enjoy these features, you will have to do the following:

  • Create an account
  • Link your account to Strava
  • Go out and bag some peaks
  • View your adventures
